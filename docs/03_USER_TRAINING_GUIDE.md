## Welcome to CLT Synapse!

This guide will walk every team member through the system step-by-step. Each section is written for a specific role. Find your role below and follow along.

**ERP URL**: `https://your-clt-synapse-url.com`

# PART 1: Getting Started (ALL Users)

## 1.1 How to Login

1. Open your browser and go to the CLT Synapse URL
2. You will see the **Login Page** with the CLT Synapse logo and "Every action, intelligently connected" tagline
3. Enter your **Email Address** (your @clt-academy.com email)
4. Enter your **Password** (provided by your admin)
5. Click the blue **"Sign In"** button
6. You will land on the **Home Dashboard**

> **Forgot password?** Click "Forgot Password?" below the Sign In button. If that doesn't work, ask your admin to reset it.

## 1.2 The Home Dashboard

After login, you'll see:
- **Welcome message** with your name and role (e.g., "Welcome, Aleesha! Sales Executive")
- **Quick Stats Bar**: Active Pipeline, New Leads Today, Enrolled MTD, Total Students, Pending Activation, Mentor Students
- **Module Cards**: Click any card to jump directly to that module (Sales, Customer Service, HR, etc.)
- **Claret AI** (purple circle, bottom-left) — your AI work companion. Click it anytime!

## 1.3 The Sidebar

The left sidebar is your main navigation. **You will only see sections relevant to your role.**

- Click a **section header** (e.g., "Sales") to expand it
- Click a **page name** to navigate there
- Your current page is highlighted in the sidebar
- At the bottom: your name, role badge, and the Claret AI icon

## 1.4 Using Claret AI (Your Smart Assistant)

Claret is available on EVERY page — look for the **purple circle** at the bottom-left corner.

**What Claret can do:**
- Answer questions: "How many leads do I have this month?"
- Set reminders: "Remind me at 3pm to follow up with Ahmed"
- Give sales advice: "How do I handle the price objection?"
- Check your stats: "What's my conversion rate?"
- Search the web: "What's happening with EUR/USD today?"

**How to use:**
1. Click the **purple circle** (bottom-left)
2. Type your message in the chat box
3. Press Enter or click Send
4. Claret responds in your preferred language (English/Hinglish/Manglish)

> **Pro tip**: Claret will proactively message you if you have hot leads going cold (untouched for 48+ hours) or missed reminders!

# PART 2: Sales Executive Guide

You manage leads through the pipeline from first contact to enrollment.

## 2.1 Viewing Your Leads

1. Click **"Sales"** in the sidebar → Click **"Sales CRM"**
2. By default, you see **"My Leads"** tab — these are leads assigned to you
3. You'll see the **Kanban Board** with 6 columns:

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **New Lead** | Fresh lead, not yet contacted |
| **Call Back** | Spoke to them, scheduled a callback |
| **Warm Lead** | Interested, needs nurturing |
| **Hot Lead** | Ready to buy, needs closing push |
| **Rejected** | Not interested / disqualified |
| **Enrolled** | Closed! Student enrolled |

4. Each lead card shows: Name, Phone, Assigned Agent, Date, SLA badge

## 2.2 Moving a Lead Through the Pipeline

1. **Drag the lead card** from one column to another (e.g., New Lead → Call Back)
2. A **dialog box** appears asking for details:
   - For **Call Back/Warm/Hot**: Set a follow-up date
   - For **Enrolled**: Select the course and enter the sale amount
3. Click **"Update"** to save

> **Important**: When enrolling, select the correct course from the dropdown. This determines the student's course name and your commission!

## 2.3 Adding a New Lead

1. Click the **"+ Add Lead"** button (top-right)
2. Fill in: Name, Phone, Email, Country, Source
3. Click **"Save"**
4. The lead appears in the **New Lead** column

## 2.4 Switching to Table View

1. Click the **"Table"** toggle next to "Kanban" (top of the page)
2. You'll see all leads in a sortable spreadsheet format
3. Click any column header to sort
4. Click the **phone icon** next to a lead to call them via 3CX

## 2.5 Checking Your Performance

1. Click **"Sales Dashboard"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see your monthly stats:
   - **Revenue**: Total AED closed this month
   - **Total Leads**: How many leads you have
   - **Conversion Rate**: % of leads that enrolled
   - **Average Deal Size**: Average enrollment amount
3. Scroll down for charts: Top agents, team revenue, trends

## 2.6 Viewing Your Commissions

1. Click **"My Commissions"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see:
   - **Sales Earned**: Commission already approved by CEO
   - **Sales Pending**: Commission awaiting CEO approval
3. The monthly trend chart shows your earnings over time

> **Note**: Commissions are calculated automatically from your enrolled leads. The CEO must approve them monthly before they move from "Pending" to "Earned".

## 2.7 Today's Follow-ups

1. Click **"Today's Follow-ups"** in the sidebar
2. See all leads with follow-up dates for today
3. Click the phone icon to call, or click the lead to update status

# PART 3: Team Leader Guide

As a Team Leader, you have everything a Sales Executive has, PLUS team visibility.

## 3.1 Viewing Your Team's Leads

1. Go to **Sales CRM** → Click **"Team Overview"** tab (red button at the top)
2. You'll see ALL leads for your team members
3. Use the **agent filter** dropdown to view a specific agent's leads
4. Monitor who has SLA warnings (red "SLA" badges)

## 3.2 Team Performance

1. Go to **Sales Dashboard**
2. Use the **filter dropdown** (top-right) to select "Overall" or your specific team
3. Review:
   - Which agents are closing the most?
   - Which agents need coaching? (low conversion rate)
   - Team-wise revenue comparison

## 3.3 Your Team's Commissions

1. Go to **My Commissions**
2. You'll see your **TL Earned** amount — this is commission from your team's sales
3. Your TL commission is calculated from all team members' enrolled deals (no 18K benchmark required)

# PART 5: Customer Service Guide (CS Agent & CS Head)

You manage students from enrollment through their learning journey.

## 5.1 Your Student Kanban

1. Click **"Customer Service"** in the sidebar → **"Customer Service"** (first item)
2. You'll see the **Student Kanban** with 5 columns:

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **New Student** | Just enrolled, needs activation call |
| **Activated** | First class attended, onboarding done |
| **Satisfactory Call** | Quality check call completed |
| **Pitched Upgrade** | Suggested course upgrade |
| **Upgraded** | Student upgraded to advanced course |

3. **My Students** tab shows only YOUR assigned students
4. **Team Overview** tab (CS Head only) shows all agents' students

## 5.2 Student Detail

1. **Click on a student card** to open the detail modal
2. You'll see **3 tabs**:
   - **Profile**: Name, phone, email, course, mentor, classes
   - **Activity**: Notes, status changes, calls made
   - **Transaction History**: All payments, upgrades, amounts

## 5.3 Moving Students Through Stages

1. **Drag the student card** from one column to the next
2. Or click the card → use the stage dropdown to update
3. Add notes about the call/interaction

## 5.4 Student Directory

1. Click **"Student Directory"** in the sidebar
2. Search any student by name, phone, or email
3. Filter by stage (New Student, Activated, etc.) or by CS Agent
4. See course name and enrollment amount for each student

## 5.5 CS Dashboard

1. Click **"CS Dashboard"** in the sidebar
2. View your performance metrics: activation rate, upgrade conversion
3. See your commission summary directly (no need to navigate away)

## 5.6 Merge Approvals (CS Head Only)

1. Click **"Merge Approvals"**
2. When duplicate student records are found, they appear here for manual review
3. Select which record to keep and merge

# PART 6: Marketing Team Guide

## 6.1 Content Calendar

This is your daily command center for managing posts across all CLT social media pages.

### Adding Your Pages
1. Go to **Marketing** → **Content Calendar**
2. Click **"Pages"** tab
3. Click **"+ Add Page"** button
4. Fill in:
   - **Page Name**: e.g., "Aqib's Personal Page"
   - **Platform**: Instagram, X, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn
   - **Handle**: e.g., @aqib_clt
   - **Posting Frequency**: Daily, Alternate Day, Twice a Week, Weekly, Biweekly
   - **Description**: What this page is about (helps AI generate relevant content)
5. Click **"Add Page"** — it gets auto-assigned a unique color

### Generating the Calendar
1. After adding ALL your pages, click **"Generate Calendar"** (top-right)
2. The system creates 30 days of entries automatically based on each page's frequency
3. Switch to **"Calendar"** tab to see the month view
4. Each day shows color-coded entries for which pages need posts

### Managing Content Pipeline
1. Click on any **calendar entry** to open the detail dialog
2. You'll see the **pipeline bar**: Planned → Video Shot → Edited → Approved → Posted
3. Click each stage button to advance the status as work progresses:
   - **Planned**: Content idea exists
   - **Video Shot**: Video has been recorded
   - **Edited**: Video/image is edited
   - **Approved**: Manager approved the final version
   - **Posted**: Published on the platform
4. Add a **title** and **description** to each entry
5. Select **Content Type**: Static Post, Reel, Carousel, Story, Video, Live

### Getting AI Content Suggestions
1. Open a calendar entry
2. Click **"Get Suggestion"** button
3. Claret AI generates:
   - **Title**: Catchy headline
   - **Hook**: First line that grabs attention
   - **Content Idea**: What the post should be about
   - **Caption**: Full ready-to-use caption with hashtags
   - **CTA**: Call to action
4. Click **"Copy Caption"** to copy directly to clipboard

### Pipeline Tab
1. Click **"Pipeline"** tab
2. See the overall status: How many posts are Planned vs Video Shot vs Edited vs Approved vs Posted
3. Per-page progress bars show which pages are on track
4. The Kanban board at the bottom groups all entries by status

> **Pro Tip**: You'll get automatic **Claret notifications** when posts are due in 48 hours but aren't "Approved" yet!

## 6.2 Competitor Intelligence

1. Go to **Marketing** → **Competitor Intel**
2. View 6 tracked competitors with their websites and social links
3. Click **"Scrape All"** to refresh data from competitor websites
4. Click a competitor card to see their **AI Battle Card** — strengths, weaknesses, and counter-strategies

## 6.3 Market Analysis

1. Go to **Marketing** → **Market Analysis**
2. **Scoring Matrix**: See CLT vs all competitors across 8 dimensions (1-10 scores)
3. **Review Sentiment**: Analyze Google Reviews for each competitor
4. **FB Ad Analysis**: See what ads competitors are running
5. **Social Intel**: Engagement signals from their social pages

## 6.4 Content Studio

1. Go to **Marketing** → **Content Studio**
2. Select a **focus area**: Social Media, Paid Ads, Email, Counter Competitors, etc.
3. Click **"Generate Content"**
4. Get AI-generated content ideas, campaign themes, messaging strategies, and a weekly calendar

# PART 9: CEO / Admin Guide

## 9.1 Executive Dashboard

1. Go to **Home** — you see the main dashboard with quick stats
2. Click **"Operations"** section for the Executive Dashboard
3. View: Revenue KPIs, department breakdown, team mood scores, attendance

## 9.2 Approving Commissions

1. Go to **My Commissions** → **"Approve Transactions"** tab
2. Review pending commission amounts per agent
3. Click **"Approve"** for each month's commissions
4. Once approved, agents see their commissions as "Earned"
5. Use **"Revoke Approval"** if needed

## 9.3 Commission Engine

1. Go to **Settings** → **Commission Engine**
2. View and edit commission rates per course
3. **Course Commissions** tab: Set commission percentages for Sales Executive, TL, SM per course
4. Changes here affect all future commission calculations

## 9.4 Admin Settings

1. Click **"Admin Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Manage: Users, roles, system settings
3. Create new users, reset passwords, activate/deactivate accounts

## 9.5 Environment Switcher

As an admin, you'll see a dropdown in the top-right showing "production / testing / development"
- **Production**: Live data — always use this
- **Testing/Development**: Only visible to admins, for development purposes
- Regular users do NOT see this switcher
